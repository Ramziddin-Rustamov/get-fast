## 2. What Data We Collect

**2.1. Account data:** first name, last name, patronymic, phone number, email (if provided), password (encrypted), profile photo.

**2.2. Location data:** region, district, neighbourhood (mahalla), home address and the pickup point coordinates of passengers (latitude/longitude).

**2.3. Driver documents:** driving licence, passport/ID, vehicle documents and photos.

**2.4. Trip and booking data:** routes, dates, number of seats, passenger lists, booking history.

**2.5. Payment data:** wallet balance, transaction history, bank card data. Full card numbers and sensitive data are securely processed through the **Hamkorbank** payment infrastructure; the Platform does not store the full card number.

**2.6. Technical data:** device type, app version, selected language, usage logs.

## 3. Purposes of Data Use

We use data for the following purposes:
- creating an account and authentication (SMS verification);
- organizing trips and bookings and connecting Driver and Passenger;
- processing payments, refunds and withdrawals;
- verifying drivers and ensuring safety;
- improving the service, support and sending notifications;
- preventing fraud and violations;
- complying with legal requirements.

## 4. Legal Basis for Processing

Data is processed based on the User's consent, the necessity to perform a contract with the User, the Operator's legitimate interests and the requirements of applicable law.

## 5. Sharing Data with Third Parties

We do not sell personal data. Data may be shared in the following cases:
- **between Driver and Passenger** — data necessary to carry out the trip (name, phone, pickup address);
- **payment service provider (Hamkorbank)** — to process payments and transfers;
- **SMS provider** — to send verification and notification codes;
- **government authorities** — when required by law or upon a lawful request.

## 6. Location Data

Pickup point coordinates are used only to organize the trip and help the Driver locate the passenger. Location data is stored in connection with the booking.

## 7. Payment Data Security

Card operations are carried out through the banking infrastructure over encrypted channels. The Platform does not store the full card number or sensitive data (CVV).

## 8. Data Retention Period

Personal data is retained for as long as necessary to provide the service and for the period required by law (e.g. financial records). After this period, data is deleted or anonymized.

## 9. Security Measures

We apply technical and organizational measures (encryption, access control, password hashing) to protect data from unauthorized access, alteration or loss. However, no method on the internet can guarantee 100% security.
